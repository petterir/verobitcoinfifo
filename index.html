Crypto FIFO tax calculator

Finnish tax reporting (Vero) — FIFO method

How to use this calculator: Enter every crypto purchase and sale you've made. You need both — purchases establish your cost basis (using the market price at the time), and sales trigger the tax calculation. You don't owe tax when you buy, only when you sell.

Traded one crypto for another? That counts as a taxable sale in Finland. Enter it as a sell of the coin you gave up (at its EUR market value on that day), then a buy of the coin you received.

What do these numbers mean?
Cost basis — The market price you paid per coin at the time of purchase, multiplied by the number of coins sold (FIFO order). Deducted from your proceeds to calculate your gain or loss when HMO does not apply.
FIFO — "First in, first out." Finnish tax rules require that your oldest coins are treated as sold first. This affects your cost basis and how long each coin was held.
Held (days) — How long the oldest coins in that sale had been held. The holding period affects whether the acquisition cost presumption can give you a better deduction.
Acquisition cost presumption (hankintameno-olettama, HMO) — An alternative to using your actual purchase price. Finnish law lets you deduct 20% of the sale price as your cost instead of what you really paid (40% if you held for 10+ years). This is useful if your records are incomplete, or if it simply results in less tax. The calculator applies it automatically when it helps you.
Capital loss — If you sold for less than you paid, you made a loss. Losses can be deducted against capital gains for up to 5 years. Report them in OmaVero even if you have no gains this year.
Tax rates — Finland taxes capital gains at 30% on the first €30,000 and 34% above that.
Import from CSV

Paste CSV with columns: type, date, asset, amount, price, total_eur (and optional notes). First row = header. price = market price per unit excl. fees — used for all gain calculations. total_eur = actual amount paid/received — used only as a fallback when price is not provided.
